# TASK-12: Research Acceleration Program

## Summary

Create a standing research workstream that continuously scans, triages, reproduces, and operationalizes relevant advances in reasoning, memory, verification, and self-improvement.

## Why This Is Necessary

The field is moving quickly. A static implementation plan will decay unless research is continuously translated into bounded experiments.

## Scope

- recurring literature review cadence
- paper triage rubric
- reproduction notebook or spike path
- implementation recommendation packets

## Deliverables

1. research backlog with priority tags,
2. paper triage rubric,
3. monthly synthesis note,
4. experiment proposals tied to ChiseAI metrics.

## Best Practices

- prioritize techniques with verifiable outcomes,
- reject hype without implementation path,
- tie every research item to a measurable ChiseAI problem,
- prefer primary sources and recent evidence.

## Hardening Requirements

- no roadmap mutation from research alone,
- research must become a bounded experiment before implementation,
- keep source provenance and publication dates.

## Telemetry

- `research_items_triaged_total`
- `research_spikes_completed_total`
- `research_to_experiment_conversion_rate`
- `research_to_prod_promotion_rate`

## Quantified Success

- at least one validated research-derived experiment per cycle,
- low-value paper churn remains low,
- implemented research shows measurable lift or is rejected with evidence.

## Research Seed Topics

1. verifier-guided reasoning and process supervision
2. self-reflection and episodic memory loops
3. graph memory and hybrid retrieval
4. evolutionary search with automated evaluators
5. self-play and self-generated supervision under strict verifier control
